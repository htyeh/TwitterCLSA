-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMMENTS ON MULTILINGUAL EXPERIMENTS WITH ES
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
** if multilingual data used in tune-step, same issues of catastrophic forgetting for En is observed, these effects are however less obvious after fine-tuning (in cases of add-Pt and add-all, they do not repair as well as in the De case)
** if multilingual data used in train-step, En scores actually constantly impove after fine-tuning with Es, by up to 8%
** Es scores are improved by about 1-1.5% by using multilingual data in train or tune steps
